FAQ

Why are you using AngularJS instead of Angular?

Angular was still an early alpha during the development of ngBolt, so we chose to continue development using AngularJS for the initial release.

Will ngBolt be updated to support to Angular?

Since AngularJS still has a higher adoption rate than Angular, an update is currently not planned. In a few years when the Angular community grows, ngBolt will likely make the switch to Angular as well as dropping support for AngularJS.
